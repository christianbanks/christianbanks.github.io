Christian Banks' Personal Profile

Hello, I am an aspiring artist who is looking to pursue a career in graphic arts. My hobbies are sketching, browsing the internet, and playing video games. I am very passionate about art and love experimenting with different forms of art, such as painting and printmaking. I am always looking to improve and take inspiration from others.

I was born in November 27, 1996 and was raised in Baton Rouge, Louisiana. In 2015, I placed 10th place in Loisiana's George Rodrigues Art Contest with my drawing, "The Bayou Jazz". To contact me, message me on my email address at: christianbanks@mybrcc.edu
